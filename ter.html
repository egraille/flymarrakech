What is paragliding?

Unlike parachute, paragliding allows us to fly in the air like birds. During the paragliding experience, we foot launch from mountain top and we fly following the winds to stay in the air and offer you the most incredible experience and views.

Frequently asked questions

  • How long will I fly?
    We offer a paragliding tandem flight that lasts between 10 to 20 minutes actually it lasts as long as you want.
  • Where are we?
    We are located 45km South of Marrakech next to Lalla Takerkouste. Please refer to the Google maps in the Contact section.
  • Health issues?
    Generally speaking, we ask to have healthy passenger. In doubt, we recommend you to bring a medical certificate showing that you can do this activity safely and ensure safety for our team.
  • Is there offers and discounts?
    Yes, we have permanent offers for groupes of 4 people and more and we run occasional commercial events. Contact us for more information. 
  • Is there an age and weight limit?
    We are limited by our equipment to a passenger between 20 to 100kg. We can take child from 6 years old. 
  • When are we open? 
    We are open everyday following the weather forecast. We will inform you about the time according to the season and we will inform you the day before if we will be flying.
